# Prompt Draft

Create a clean 4:3 horizontal WeChat article illustration in minimalist `notion` style.

Show a central node labeled `AI`, connected to three surrounding domains: `工作`, `学习`, `生活`.
Above the central structure, show a clear progression of three role labels:
`工具箱` -> `搭子` -> `伙伴`.

The image should communicate that AI is no longer a distant technology concept, but has already entered daily life and is becoming a practical companion.

Visual requirements:
- minimalist editorial infographic
- hand-drawn line feel, but neat and restrained
- off-white background
- black or dark gray lines
- light neutral blocks and subtle blue-gray accents
- Chinese labels only
- no realistic humans
- clear hierarchy and plenty of whitespace
- suitable for WeChat article body

Avoid:
- futuristic sci-fi glow
- dense UI panels
- over-decorated icons
- poster-like dramatic contrast
