# Prompt Draft

Create a 4:3 horizontal infographic for a WeChat article in minimalist `notion` style.

Center concept: `普通人 + AI`.

Around the center, arrange six clean lifestyle capability modules with Chinese labels:
- `做饭`
- `修图`
- `旅行攻略`
- `路线规划`
- `短视频`
- `封面设计`

The illustration should express that AI is helping ordinary people gain practical capabilities in daily life, not just helping experts become stronger.

Visual requirements:
- modular infographic layout
- light hand-drawn line quality
- clear spacing and visual rhythm
- soft neutral blocks, subtle accent colors
- readable Chinese typography
- polished but understated

Avoid:
- app screenshot collage feel
- loud colors
- too many tiny icons
