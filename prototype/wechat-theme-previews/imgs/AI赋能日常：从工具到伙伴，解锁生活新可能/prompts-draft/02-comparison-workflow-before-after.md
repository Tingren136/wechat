# Prompt Draft

Create a 4:3 horizontal comparison illustration for a WeChat article in minimalist `notion` style.

Split the composition into left and right panels.

Left panel theme: `传统重复劳动`
- a tired office worker
- surrounded by scattered tasks: `周报`, `会议纪要`, `改文案`, `做汇总`
- visual feeling of clutter, repetition, and time drain

Right panel theme: `AI 协作`
- the same person now calmer and more focused
- person handles `判断` and `方向`
- AI assistant handles `整理`, `提炼`, `润色`, `归纳`
- visual feeling of smoother workflow and lighter mental load

Style requirements:
- clean line art
- restrained monochrome with a few muted accent colors
- modern productivity illustration, not cartoonish
- simple Chinese labels
- high readability inside a long-form article

Avoid:
- exaggerated facial expressions
- corporate stock illustration style
- busy software dashboards
