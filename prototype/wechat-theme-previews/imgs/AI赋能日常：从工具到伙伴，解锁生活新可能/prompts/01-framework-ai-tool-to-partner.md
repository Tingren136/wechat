# Prompt

Create a clean 4:3 horizontal WeChat article illustration in minimalist notion style.

Main concept:
- central node labeled `AI`
- three connected surrounding domains labeled `工作`, `学习`, `生活`
- above the structure, show a clear progression:
  `工具箱` -> `搭子` -> `伙伴`

The image should express that AI is no longer a distant technology concept, but has already entered everyday life and is becoming a practical companion.

Visual direction:
- minimalist editorial framework diagram
- subtle hand-drawn line quality
- off-white background
- dark gray or black lines
- light neutral blocks with restrained blue-gray accents
- Chinese labels only
- no realistic people
- plenty of whitespace
- highly readable in WeChat article body

Avoid:
- sci-fi glowing effects
- dense dashboards
- dramatic poster composition
- overloaded icons
