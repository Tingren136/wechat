# Prompt

Create a 4:3 horizontal scene illustration for a WeChat article in minimalist notion style.

Show a calm learning environment.
A learner sits at a desk, slightly puzzled but engaged.
On a nearby screen or floating cards, an AI tutor offers multiple forms of help with Chinese labels:
- `换一种方式讲`
- `再讲一遍`
- `拆重点`
- `练口语`

The emotional message:
AI makes patient, private-tutor-like guidance accessible to ordinary people.

Visual requirements:
- soft and quiet composition
- clean lines and restrained shapes
- warm neutral background
- very light accent colors
- no flashy interface
- modern educational editorial feeling

Avoid:
- childish cartoon style
- dominant robot mascot
- overly realistic 3D rendering
