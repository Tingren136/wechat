# Prompt

Create a 4:3 horizontal infographic for a WeChat article in minimalist notion style.

Center concept: `普通人 + AI`

Around the center, arrange six clean capability modules with Chinese labels:
- `做饭`
- `修图`
- `旅行攻略`
- `路线规划`
- `短视频`
- `封面设计`

The image should express that AI helps ordinary people gain practical everyday capabilities, not just making experts stronger.

Visual requirements:
- modular infographic layout
- light hand-drawn line quality
- clear spacing and rhythm
- soft neutral blocks with subtle accents
- polished but understated
- readable Chinese typography

Avoid:
- screenshot collage feeling
- loud colors
- too many tiny icons
