# Prompt

Create a 4:3 horizontal conceptual comparison illustration for a WeChat article in minimalist notion style.

Main structure:
- a central fork or decision split
- left branch labeled `AI 替代品`
- right branch labeled `AI 放大器`

Around the split, show three guardrails with Chinese labels:
- `隐私红线`
- `结果核查`
- `保持判断`

The image should convey that the real risk is not only AI mistakes, but users giving up their own judgment.

Visual requirements:
- concept-driven editorial diagram
- restrained line work
- strong visual clarity
- off-white background, dark lines, muted accents
- suitable for a reflective opinion section

Avoid:
- alarmist warning signs
- hacker or cyberpunk aesthetics
- crowded text blocks
